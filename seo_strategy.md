# SEO Strategy

## In scope
- Public marketing pages in `artifacts/marketing`
- Public crawl files for the marketing site (`robots.txt`, `sitemap.xml`, favicon assets)
- Public platform entry/auth pages only where they are directly crawlable (`/platform/`, `/platform/sign-in`, `/platform/sign-up`)

## Out of scope
- Authenticated platform application routes under `/platform/app/**`
- Admin and internal tooling routes
- API endpoints except where they directly affect crawlability or indexation

## Target audience
- Institutional investors
- Investment analysts
- Asset managers, hedge funds, private equity, and investment banking teams

## Primary keywords
- AI financial intelligence platform
- financial research platform
- institutional investor intelligence
- AI for investment research
